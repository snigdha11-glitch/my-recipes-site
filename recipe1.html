Corn Cheese Sandwich

Difficulty- Easy

Total Time- 10 mins

INGREDIENTS :

Corn Filling

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sweet corn, boiled
  • 2 tbsp capsicum, chopped
  • 2 tbsp red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1-2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup mozzarella, grated
  • 2 cube processed cheese, grated
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp red chilli flakes
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried parsley
  • 1/2 tsp chaat masala

Assemble

  • 6 slices of bread
  • 1 tbsp green chutney per sandwich
  • sweet corn filling
  • butter to toast

INSTRUCTIONS :

  1. In a bowl, add boiled sweet corn, finely chopped onion and chopped red and green bell peppers.
  2. Add lots of grated mozzarella and processed cheese, followed by a spoonful of mayonnaise.
  3. Season with salt, black pepper, oregano, chilli flakes, dried parsley and chaat masala.
  4. Mix everything really well until you have a creamy filling.
  5. Take two slices of bread and spread green chutney on one slice.
  6. Top generously with the corn and cheese filling and close with the other slice of bread.
  7. Toast the sandwich in butter on a pan until golden brown and crisp on both sides.
  8. Slice in half and enjoy!
