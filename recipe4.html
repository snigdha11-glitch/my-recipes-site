Difficulty - Easy

Total Time - 25 mins

INGREDIENTS :

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

INSTRUCTIONS :

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Cream butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until fluffy.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla extract, mix well.
  4. Sift flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Combine with wet mixture.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop spoonfuls onto tray and bake for 10–12 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Cool on wire rack and enjoy.
